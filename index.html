Visit Owerri, Imo

Visit the Eastern Heartland, Owerri to immerse yourself in its rich culture, history, and scenic beauty. The most vibrant city in Nigeria, full of adventure and relaxation.

Experience breath taking views at Okigwe Rolling Hills

A perfect spot for hiking, site seeing, and connecting with the beauty of nature.

Visit the serene Oguta Lake

A natural fresh water lake known for it crystal-clear waters and scenic surroundings.

Marvel at the Grandeur of Assumpta Cathedral

One of the largest Catholic church in West Africa.

Discover the stories of Nigeria's greatest heroes at Hero Square

This expansive land mark serves as a memorial to historical figures.
